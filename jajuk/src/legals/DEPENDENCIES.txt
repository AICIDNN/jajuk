Jajuk Dependencies
==================
The OFFICIAL list of API, frameworks or packaging tools Jajuk includes can be 
found on http://jajuk.info/index.php/jajuk_dependencies 

NOTE TO JAJUK DEVS: DO NOT EDIT THIS FILE. The text bellow is manually keep up
 to date  from http://jajuk.info/index.php/jajuk_dependencies
