The Help view

This view provides Jajuk help system. For the moment, only two languages are available: English and French (voluntary translators are welcomed).

It uses JavaHelp solution (http://java.sun.com/products/javahelp/). Basically, it features:

  • A control panel at the upper part to browse (previous/next) documentation, to print it (print and printer configuration) and to add current page as a favorite.
  • A table of contents with ordered subjects. Click on the subject to display it in the right window.
  • A search panel to find pages which matches a searched term.
  • A favorite panel with your favorites pages (stored under {your home directory}/.JavaHelp)
