Keystrokes

Most of Jajuk functions can be controled using keyboard. Keystrokes are:

Player

  • [Control-p]: Play / Pause
  • [Control-s]: Stop
  • [Control-t]: Switch in and out Repeat mode
  • [Control-h]: Switch in and out Shuffle mode
  • [Control-Alt-Left]: Seek rewind in current track
  • [Control-Left]: Play previous track
  • [Control-Maj-Left]: Play previous album
  • [Control-Alt-Right]: Seek forward in current track
  • [Control-Right]: Play next track
  • [Control-Maj-Right]: Play next album

Sound controls

  • [F8]: Mute
  • [Control-Down]: Increase gain
  • [Control-Up]: Decrease gain

Others

  • [F1]: Help
