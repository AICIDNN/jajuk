The Help view

This view provides Jajuk help system. For the moment, only two languages are available : English and French (volontary translators are welcomed).

It uses JavaHelp solution (http://java.sun.com/products/javahelp/). Basicaly, it features under Jajuk :

  • A control panel at the top to browse (previous/next) documentation, to print it (print and printer configuration) and to add current page as a favorite.
  • A table of contents with ordered subjects. Click on the subject to  display it in the right window.
  • A search panel to find pages which matches searched word.
  • A favorite panel with your favorites pages (stored under {your home directory}/.JavaHelp)
